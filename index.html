Lightweight And Full Featured

Built-in Major Solving Algorithms

QtMind is a mastermind board game with several playing modes and built-in solving engine. Coded in C++ and Qt, it is very fast and lightweight.

All major solving algorithms are implemented in QtMind, namely, Donald Knuth's Worst Case, the Most Parts algorithm, and the Expected Size algorithms.

The graphical elements mimics that of ColorCode, another perfect mastermind clone, although it is completely rewritten is a simpler coding, reducing the final output to almost %100 improvement in size and preformance.
