Light Weight And Full Futured

Built In Major Solving Algorithms

Codebreak is a mastermind computer game with several playing mode and built in solving engine. Coded in C++ with the aid of Qt, it is very fast and light weight. The binary is about 3 megabyte on linux.

All major solving algorithms are implemented in Codebreak, namely, Donald Knuth's Worst Case, the Most Parts, the Expected Size.

The graphical elements mimics that of ColorCode, another perfect mastermind clone, although it is completely rewritten is a simpler coding, reducing the final output to %500 improvement over ColorCode.
